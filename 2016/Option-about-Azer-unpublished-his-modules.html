Option about Azer unpublished his modules

Posted on 2016-03-23   |  

I have got the message that Azer Koçulu just unpublished ALL his Modules from NPM. the reason is a company asked Azer Koçulu to delete his module name “kik” for “Kik” is a registered brand. And Isaac Z. Schlueter (npm ceo) accepted to change the ownership of that module without Azer Koçulu`s agreement. Here is the details.

From his post, I can feel that the true reason why he deleted his modules is he realized NPM is not a real open source community.

As he wrote in his essay

This situation made me realize that NPM is someone’s private land
where corporate is more powerful than the people, and I do open source
because, Power To The People.

As my option, I support him to unpublish his modules for the Spirit of open source and so does many people support him. He just wants to awake this sprit. But I also understand NPM not wanting to get embroiled in legal disputes, but they could have handled it with integrity. It was incredibly unprofessional for NPM to not even ask questions or give the author an opportunity to make changes.

As I see from Github, Plenty people have sucked in this situiation for those popular, highly indenpent modules.

I have just visited the website of Kik which has registered brand, There is no link between Kik Messenger and Kik module. Just for a name? I think Kik messenger Co. could ask Azer Koçulu to write a announcement that the non-link between the different Kik. Funny to force @izs to change the ownership.

Cheers.
